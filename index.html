About Akwaaba Restaurant

Welcome to Akwaaba Restaurant! We're proud to serve authentic Ghanaian cuisine with a modern twist. Our QR code ordering system makes it easy for you to browse our menu, place your order, and track your food preparation in real-time.

How It Works:

  1. Scan the QR code on your table
  2. Browse our delicious menu
  3. Add items to your cart
  4. Fill in your table number and contact info
  5. Submit your order and get an order number
  6. Track your order status in real-time
  7. Get notified when your food is ready!
