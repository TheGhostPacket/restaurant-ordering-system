About Akwaaba Restaurant

Welcome to Akwaaba Restaurant! We're proud to serve authentic Ghanaian cuisine with a modern twist. Our QR code ordering system makes it easy for you to browse our menu, place your order, and enjoy your meal without waiting in line.

How It Works:

  1. Scan the QR code on your table
  2. Browse our delicious menu
  3. Add items to your cart
  4. Fill in your table number and contact info
  5. Submit your order
  6. Relax while we prepare your food!
